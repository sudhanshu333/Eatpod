Immune Boosting Foods to Keep You Healthy

June 17, 2020

As Coronavirus (COVID-19) continues to impact our communities and the larger world around us, staying healthy is on the forefront of everyone’s.... minds. Simple hygiene practices such as washing hands and administering to social distance rules can go a long way, but the food we choose to eat may also have a strong effect on our continued health.
*Fresh or Frozen?
It’s really a matter of personal preference. From a nutritional point of view, you’re right….fruits and vegetables that are frozen shortly after being picked retain about as many vitamins and minerals as freshly picked and eaten fruits. Just make sure that there aren’t any other added ingredients (salt, sugar, sauces, etc.) that contribute additional calories, sodium, or fat.
The most important thing for most of us is to eat MORE fruits and veggies – of either kind! Fortunately, it’s easier than ever to get your fruits and vegetables at restaurants these days – through salads, side dishes, and even entrées.  To add more fruits and vegetables to your diet.
Look for menu items containing fruits and vegetables when you eat out.
Ask for (extra) lettuce, tomatoes, cucumbers, etc., on sandwiches, extra cabbage in burritos and tacos, etc.

Flourless Banana Oatmeal Microwavable Muffins

June 15, 2020

These gluten free banana oatmeal muffins make a perfect snack anytime of day. I love them pre-workout! They are flourless, and can be made dairy free.... with a few substitutions. If you’re anything like me, you enjoy having a homemade baked good every once in a while. But most recipes yield 12-24 servings which I don’t always want sitting on my counter tempting me! The idea of a single serving mug-muffin is a great solution, and even more-so when it’s microwavable!
Call me crazy but I love getting in my workout at the crack of dawn. I frequent the 6AM CrossFit class and occasionally make it to the 5AM. Two days a week I also coach the 6 and 7 AM classes. So what does this all have to do with healthy muffins? Well I’ve always struggled trying to figure out if/when/what I should be eating at the early hours of the day before I workout. For a long time I would workout fasted with a bit of coffee or pre-workout.
Recently I’ve been playing around a bit more with food timing and have been trying to get in a little something like a banana or an RxBar pre-workout. On days I coach I’ve discovered I have to eat something or else the second class I coach tends to get a bit more of the “hangry” side of me.
Okay so that’s all great but what about delicious, microwavable, banana muffins?! Well, they are a perfect little pre-workout snack! Just blend up all the ingredients, pour it into a mug, microwave and voila! A warm, fluffy, healthy muffin!
